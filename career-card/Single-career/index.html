Summary

We’re looking for a driven individual with a good understanding of how content marketing, digital PR and campaigns contribute to SEO success. We’re looking for somebody who isn’t afraid to let data speak for itself, but who can also be creative in their approach to content marketing campaigns.

Responsibilities

There is an opportunity to help shape our department into something much larger and our vision is for you to grow your own area of expertise and potentially build a team under you. You will need to demonstrate experience in online PR and promotional strategies which gain coverage to acquire links and mentions of campaigns – we want to see tangible results where possible. You will be account managing clients, supporting ideation and planning campaigns. The main function of this role is the planning, strategy and execution of content marketing, which will be to support SEO goals alongside the technical SEO team. This includes client management, pitching, creating ideas, organisation and planning.
